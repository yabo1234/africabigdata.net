Partnership Overview

Ethiopia is devoted to achieving the Sustainable Development Goals and reaching middle-income status by 2030 through a climate-resilient, low-carbon development path. Through the Global Gateway initiative, Team Europe provides Ethiopia with a fair, transparent, and advantageous partnership to enhance the regulatory and investment climate, elevate infrastructure development, and connect Ethiopia to a future of opportunities and prosperity.

Ethiopia's Development Vision

Central to Ethiopia's transformation is the Home-Grown Economic Reform Programme 2.0, which aims to:

  • Ensure macroeconomic stability and sustainable fiscal management
  • Improve the business climate for domestic and international investment
  • Boost competitiveness across key economic sectors
  • Enhance public sector efficiency and transparency
  • Transition from public sector-driven to private sector-led economy
  • Reduce debt burdens while raising infrastructure standards
  • Create jobs and business opportunities for inclusive growth

Four Investment Pillars

Global Gateway focuses on four strategic pillars to accelerate Ethiopia's sustainable development:

💻 Human-Centric Digital Transformation

A Competitive, Open and Inclusive Digital Economy

Global Gateway invests in infrastructure, businesses, institutions and people to accelerate Ethiopia's digital transformation. This includes:

  • Digital infrastructure development for nationwide connectivity
  • DESTA programme - Advancing Market-Ready Digital Skills
  • TVET system enhancement to power Ethiopia's growing digital economy
  • E-mobility and digital services integration
  • Digital connectivity extension across rural and urban areas

Goal: Equip Ethiopians with market-relevant digital and business skills while building robust digital infrastructure.

⚡ Clean Energy Transition for All

Clean Energy, Empowered Ethiopia

Global Gateway supports investments and rules paving the way to a clean energy transition for all in Ethiopia. Key initiatives include:

  • Grid modernization and digitalization for enhanced reliability
  • Renewable energy development - wind, solar, and hydropower
  • Regional energy hub development for cross-border energy trade
  • Clean cooking solutions and organic fertilizer access
  • Off-grid renewable energy for rural communities

Goal: Ensure clean, modern and affordable energy access for all Ethiopians while becoming a regional renewable energy hub.

🌾 Sustainable Agri-Food Value Chains

Harvesting Global Markets, Nurturing Local Futures

Global Gateway invests in the coffee and horticulture value chains, connecting Ethiopia to the EU market and boosting opportunities for smallholder farmers:

  • Cold storage infrastructure for perishable goods
  • Supply chain competitiveness enhancement
  • Export sector development for fruits and vegetables
  • Smallholder farmer support especially women and youth
  • Agro-processing and value addition capabilities

Goal: Transform agriculture from subsistence to commercial, export-oriented production while ensuring food security.

🏥 Health System Strengthening

Stronger Healthcare, Thriving Communities

Global Gateway strengthens healthcare capacities and invests in Ethiopia's health system through:

  • Hospital rehabilitation and modernization
  • Health facility electrification in rural areas
  • Medical equipment and technology upgrades
  • Healthcare workforce development
  • Referral and teaching hospital capacity building

Goal: Build a resilient healthcare system serving over 10 million people with quality medical services.

Global Gateway Flagship Projects

Transformative investments showcasing how Team Europe implements Global Gateway in Ethiopia:

⚡ RISED Ethiopia

Clean Energy Transition for All & Human-Centric Digital Transformation

From ensuring clean, modern and affordable energy access for all Ethiopians to powering e-mobility and digital infrastructure and selling decarbonised energy to neighbouring countries, the future of Ethiopia is electric!

Investment: EUR 270 million mobilized by Team Europe

Key Components:

  • Electricity transmission network rehabilitation and modernization
  • Grid digitalization for smart energy management
  • Ashegoda 120MW wind farm restoration
  • Digital connectivity extension nationwide
  • Ethiopian energy professionals capacity strengthening

Partnership: Ethiopian Electric Power and Team Europe collaboration

Impact: Making the power grid stronger, smarter and greener while supporting the twin green and digital transition.

🌪️ Assela Wind Farm

Clean Energy Transition for All

Ethiopia is rich in renewable energy resources. The Assela 100MW wind farm demonstrates Global Gateway in action, leveraging finance and expertise from Team Europe partners to build smart, clean and secure energy connections.

Capacity: 100MW wind power generation
Financed by: Denmark
Developer: Siemens Energy (European company)

Strategic Benefits:

  • Grid resilience enhancement - reducing dependence on hydropower
  • Climate change adaptation - diversifying energy sources
  • Regional energy hub development - positioning Ethiopia as renewable energy leader
  • First power delivery - turbines already feeding into national grid

Milestone: First turbines started feeding power into Ethiopia's national grid in 2025.

🚛 Cool Port Addis

Sustainable Agri-Food Value Chains

Agriculture is the backbone of the Ethiopian economy with 80% of the population depending on it. Cool Port Addis enhances supply chain competitiveness and spurs large-scale fruit and vegetable export sector growth.

Developer: Dutch Flying Swans
Co-financed by: Netherlands and Government of Ethiopia
Location: Modjo Dry Port

Project Features:

  • Cold storage facility for perishable goods
  • Addis-Djibouti railway connection for efficient transport
  • Expressway connectivity for road transport
  • Export sector development for fruits and vegetables
  • Smallholder farmer opportunities especially for women and youth

Impact: Creating significant income opportunities for smallholder farmers while contributing to food security and export diversification.

Partnership Impact

The EU-Ethiopia Global Gateway partnership delivers transformative results:

  • Economic Transformation: Supporting transition to private sector-led economy
  • Energy Security: Building resilient, diversified energy infrastructure
  • Digital Inclusion: Connecting rural and urban communities to digital opportunities
  • Agricultural Modernization: Enhancing productivity and market access for farmers
  • Healthcare Strengthening: Improving access to quality medical services
  • Skills Development: Preparing workforce for digital economy
  • Regional Integration: Positioning Ethiopia as regional hub for energy and trade
