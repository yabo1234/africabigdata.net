📚 About This Portal

An independent educational resource dedicated to documenting and explaining the relationship between the European Union and the African Union

← Back to Home

Our Mission

The EU–AU Educational Resource (africanbigdata.et) serves as a comprehensive, independent platform for understanding one of the world's most significant inter-continental partnerships. We provide accessible, accurate, and up-to-date information about the European Union, the African Union, and their evolving relationship.

"Together, the EU and AU represent over 1.8 billion people, spanning 82 nations and two continents — a partnership that shapes the global agenda on trade, climate, health, and digital governance."

What We Provide

🇪🇺 EU Overview

Comprehensive information about European Union institutions, treaties, legislation, and policy briefs. We break down complex EU structures into accessible explanations.

🌍 AU Overview

Detailed coverage of African Union governance, Agenda 2063 framework, peace & security initiatives, and economic programmes driving continental integration.

🤝 Partnership Analysis

In-depth exploration of EU-AU cooperation history, current initiatives, and future prospects across the six pillars of partnership.

🤖 AI & Digital Policy

Curated directory of 36 major African AI governance organizations, policy trackers, declarations, and research hubs.

📰 Current Developments

Latest news, policy announcements, joint declarations, and analysis of evolving EU-AU relations.

📊 Data & Statistics

Key statistics, trade figures, demographic data, and economic indicators that define the EU-AU relationship.

Our Approach

We believe that understanding complex international relationships requires clear, accessible information. Our approach is:

🎯 Independent

We maintain editorial independence while providing balanced, factual coverage of both unions and their partnership.

📚 Educational

Our content serves students, researchers, journalists, policymakers, and engaged citizens seeking to understand EU-AU relations.

🔄 Current

We continuously update our resources to reflect the latest developments, policy changes, and partnership initiatives.

🌐 Accessible

Complex policy frameworks are presented in clear, understandable language with visual aids and structured information.

Target Audience

This portal serves a diverse community of users interested in EU-AU relations:

  • Students and Researchers: Academic resources for international relations, African studies, European studies, and development policy
  • Journalists and Media: Reliable background information and current developments for reporting on EU-AU affairs
  • Policy Professionals: Comprehensive overview of institutional frameworks and policy initiatives
  • Civil Society: Information for NGOs, advocacy groups, and organizations working on EU-AU cooperation
  • Business Community: Trade and investment information relevant to EU-AU economic relations
  • Engaged Citizens: Accessible information for anyone interested in understanding this important global partnership

Partnership with AAME

We are proud to partner with AAME (African AI & Digital Policy Resources) to provide the most comprehensive directory of African AI governance organizations, policy trackers, and research hubs. This collaboration ensures our AI resources section remains current and authoritative.
